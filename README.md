#  Zomato Market Intelligence & Expansion Strategy System

##  Objective

To analyze 40,000+ Zomato restaurant records and develop a data-driven system to identify optimal locations, pricing strategies, and cuisine trends for business expansion.

##  Tech Stack

* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy)
* Data Cleaning & Preprocessing
* Feature Engineering
* Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)


##  Data Processing

* Cleaned and processed raw dataset by handling missing values and inconsistent formats
* Standardized rating and cost columns for analysis
* Engineered new features:

  * Cost per person
  * Price category (Low, Medium, High, Luxury)
  * Rating segmentation
  * Cuisine count

##  Key Analysis Performed

* **Price vs Rating Analysis** → Evaluated impact of pricing on customer satisfaction
* **Location Analysis** → Identified high-demand and high-performance areas
* **Online Ordering Impact** → Assessed effect of digital convenience
* **Cuisine Trends** → Analyzed demand vs quality across cuisines

##  Key Insights

* Higher-priced restaurants show better ratings, but with diminishing returns at luxury level
* BTM is highly saturated with high demand but lower ratings (~3.57)
* Koramangala 5th Block offers the best balance of demand and quality (~4.0 rating)
* Premium multi-cuisine restaurants achieve the highest customer satisfaction


##  Expansion Strategy Model

Developed a **data-driven scoring model** combining:

* Demand (number of restaurants)
* Customer satisfaction (ratings)
This model identifies **high-potential locations for business expansion**

## Project Files

* `zomato_analysis.ipynb` → Complete analysis and model

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can be leveraged to make strategic business decisions by combining demand analysis with quality metrics, enabling smarter expansion planning.
